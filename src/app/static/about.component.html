ToDo App as a student project

As a student at ICT College, I decided to put an extra effort and try to develop an Angular2 SPA for my final project. Technologies I picked (see the items below), are not the part of a regular curriculum, so for me it was kind of a risky journey towards unknown. But, it was worth it. I learned so much.

Although Angular2 final version is not ready yet, and I had to consequently work with multiple alpha, beta and RC versions, I was still able to grasp it's huge potential and have all intentions to dive deeper into it, along with Reactive programing in general.
